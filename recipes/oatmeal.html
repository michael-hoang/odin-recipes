Simple and Healthy Oatmeal

Simple ingredients, easy directions, and plenty of fiber and protein make this oatmeal the goto breakfast every morning! The best part is that it's customizable to your liking. Spice up your oatmeal with your favorite toppings like nuts, nut butter, fresh or dried fruits, or even jam!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of old-fashioned rolled oats
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1/8 tsp of kosher salt
  • 1/2 tsp of ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp of honey
  • Your desired toppings (slicked almonds, peanut butter, fresh fruit, etc)

Steps

  1. Combine oats, milk, water, salt, and cinnamon in a saucepan. Bring to boil, then reduce heat to low.
  2. Simmer uncovered for 3-5 minutes until thickened, stirring occasionally. Remove from heat and let cool slightly.
  3. Pour into bowl and drizzle with 1/2 teaspoon honey. Add additional desired toppings and serve.
